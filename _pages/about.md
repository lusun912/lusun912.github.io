I am currently an Associate Professor at [Tohoku University](https://www.tohoku.ac.jp/en/), Japan, affiliated with the [Unprecedented-scale Data Analytics Center](https://udac.tohoku.ac.jp/), Graduate School of Information Sciences. Since May 2026, I have also been serving as a Visiting Scientist at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project (AIP), working with the [AI Security and Privacy Team](https://www.riken.jp/en/research/labs/aip/ai_soc/ai_sec_privacy/index.html).

I received my B.S. from Wuhan University (2009), M.S. from Northeast Normal University (2013), and Dr. Eng. from Hokkaido University (2017), under the supervision of Prof. [Mineichi Kudo](https://researchmap.jp/read0166594). I was a postdoctoral researcher at Kyoto University (2017–2019, with Prof. [Hiroshi Mamitsuka](https://www.bic.kyoto-u.ac.jp/pathway/mami/)), an Assistant Professor at ShanghaiTech University (2019–2024), and a Research Scientist at RIKEN AIP (2024–2026).
