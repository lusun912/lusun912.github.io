I am currently an Associate Professor at [Tohoku University](https://www.tohoku.ac.jp/en/), Japan. I was born in September 1985 in [Zhengzhou](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zhengzhou), China. I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Wuhan University, China, in June 2009, my M.S. degree in Electronic Circuits and Systems from Northeast Normal University, China, in June 2013, and my Doctor of Engineering (Dr. Eng.) degree in Information Engineering from Hokkaido University in September 2017, under the supervision of Prof. [Mineichi Kudo](https://researchmap.jp/read0166594). From October 2017 to October 2019, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Kyoto University under the supervision of Prof. [Hiroshi Mamitsuka](https://www.bic.kyoto-u.ac.jp/pathway/mami/). I then served as an Assistant Professor at ShanghaiTech University from November 2019 to September 2024. From November 2024 to January 2026, I was a Research Scientist at RIKEN AIP in the [AI Security and Privacy Team](https://www.riken.jp/en/research/labs/aip/ai_soc/ai_sec_privacy/index.html). In February 2026, I joined Tohoku University as an Associate Professor at the [Unprecedented-scale Data Analytics Center](https://udac.tohoku.ac.jp/).
